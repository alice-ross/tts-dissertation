Section 2: Listening and recall

Instruction screen

This study requires the use of audio through your device's speakers and/or headphones. You'll be asked to listen to some short recordings and answer a few questions about what you heard. It should take around 5 minutes.

Please make sure that you can hear audio from your device clearly. Adjust volume if necessary, and use a headset or earphones if you can. You should not have any other loud sounds (e.g. music or TV) nearby.

Please don't use the Back or Refresh buttons on your browser during the experiment.

At the end you will receive a redirect link which will take you back to Prolific to receive your payment.

To continue, please type the code that you hear.
